About Lovee’s House

Lovee's House was born in 2012, a year marked by the arrival of Pharaoh's formerly known as Lynn Phillips’ first grandchild, lovingly nicknamed Angel. Over the years, Pharaoh's family has grown, with 9 angels joining her circle of love and inspiration. For over a decade, the phrase “I want to go to Lovee’s House” has echoed through schools, stores, homes, and communities, embodying a feeling of joy, comfort, and belonging.

These angels became more than family; they became Pharaoh's guiding light, revealing her true purpose. Inspired by their love and the happiness they found in her home, Pharaoh realized she was meant to extend that love to others. For years, she has been a source of strength in her community, offering counseling, housing, financial assistance, mental rest, and encouragement to those in need.

During a moment of meditation and reflection, Pharaoh sought clarity about her purpose. She heard the phrase that had become so familiar: “I want to go to Lovee’s House.” When she asked what it meant, the answer was clear: just as her grandchildren cherished being in her presence, so did the collective. It was a call to extend her gifts to a larger community.

Lovee’s House is more than a place – it’s a movement. It’s a sanctuary for support, a hub for guidance, and a source of inspiration. Here, Pharaoh Ma’at Ka Ra offers heart, wisdom, and unwavering commitment to making a difference.

Whether you’re seeking a moment of rest, a word of encouragement, or simply a place to feel at home, Lovee’s House is here for you.

Lovee’s House – Where love lives and community thrives.
