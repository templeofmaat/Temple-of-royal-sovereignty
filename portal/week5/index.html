üïØÔ∏è Daily Reverence Ritual

Morning Practice (15 minutes):

  1. Light a candle or face the rising sun
  2. Speak gratitude for three things
  3. Honor one ancestor by name
  4. Set intention: "I walk in reverence today"

Evening Practice (10 minutes):

  1. Reflect on where you showed reverence
  2. Note where reverence was lacking
  3. Offer forgiveness to self and others
  4. Give thanks for the day's lessons
