üïäÔ∏è The Purity Ritual (Day 7)

Step 1: Preparation

Create a sacred space. Light a candle. Have paper and pen ready.

Step 2: Declaration Reading

Read all 6 Truth Declarations aloud. After each, say "This I have mastered" or "This I am mastering."

Step 3: Written Confession

Write one truth you've been avoiding. Write one falsehood you've been living. Then burn the paper safely.

Step 4: Truth Vow

Speak aloud: "I vow to speak truth, live truth, and be truth. My word is sacred. My life is authentic."

Step 5: Integration

Sit in silence for 7 minutes. Feel truth integrating into every cell of your being.
