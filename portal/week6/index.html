Each day this week, do one thing that scares you but aligns with your truth:

Day 1: Speak a difficult truth to someone

Day 2: Set a clear boundary you've been avoiding

Day 3: Express appreciation to someone you admire

Day 4: Share a creative work with someone

üîÑ Fear Transformation Practice

When fear arises:

  1. Notice it without judgment
  2. Breathe into the fear
  3. Ask: "What truth wants to be expressed here?"
  4. Take one small action toward that truth

Record each fear and transformation in your Courage Journal.
